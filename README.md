## What is CrypTok?

CrypTok™ is a next-generation **cryptocurrency-integrated social media platform** that combines the best of traditional social networking with Web3 technology. Built for the crypto community, by the crypto community.

Unlike traditional social platforms, CrypTok puts **creators first** with built-in monetization tools, direct crypto tipping, and a native token economy that rewards engagement.

- **Rich Content Creation** - Posts, photos, videos, articles, reels, and stories
- **Real-Time Chat** - WebSocket-powered instant messaging with typing indicators
- **Live Streaming** - HD broadcasts with co-hosting, virtual gifts, and super chat
- **Communities** - Pages, groups, events, and forums for every interest
- **Discovery** - Trending posts, hashtags, and personalized recommendations
